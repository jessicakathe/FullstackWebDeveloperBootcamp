HTML5 Formatting Tags

Bold text is often used to highlight keywords or important concepts.

Strong text indicates that something is of high importance or urgency.

Italic text is typically used to denote a technical term, a phrase from another language, or a thought.

Emphasized text is similar to italic but with semantic importance, often read with stress in a screen reader.

Marked text highlights parts of text for reference, similar to a highlighter pen on paper.

Smaller text is used for fine print or side comments.

Deleted text shows content that has been removed from the document.

Inserted text indicates additions to the document.

This is subscript text, used for chemical formulas like H2O.

This is superscript text, used for footnotes or mathematical exponents like x2.
